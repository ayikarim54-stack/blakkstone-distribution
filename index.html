FAQ
Common questions
How long does it take for my music to go live?
Typically 3–10 business days depending on the platform. Spotify and Apple Music usually take 3–5 days. You'll receive an email once your release is live.
How do I get paid?
You can request payouts via MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money, or direct bank transfer. All payments are processed in Ghana Cedis (GHS) within 5–7 business days of your request.
What is the 9% commission on the Solo plan?
On the free Solo plan, Blakkstone keeps 15% of your royalties to cover distribution infrastructure and costs. Upgrade to Artist+ ($25/year) or Label ($45/year) to keep 100% of everything you earn.
Can I distribute compilations or Various Artists albums?
Yes. Our submission wizard supports both standard Artist/Band releases and Compilation/Various Artists albums. You can add multiple primary and featured artists per track.
What audio formats are accepted?
We accept WAV, MP3 (320kbps minimum), and FLAC files. For best quality on all platforms we recommend 16-bit, 44.1kHz stereo WAV files.
Do I keep my rights when I distribute through Blakkstone?
Absolutely. You retain 100% ownership of your master recordings and compositions. Blakkstone only acts as a distribution channel — your music always belongs to you.
Can I remove or update a release after it's live?
Yes. Contact our support team via the dashboard messages and we'll process update requests (metadata corrections, artwork changes) or takedowns within 3–5 business days.
